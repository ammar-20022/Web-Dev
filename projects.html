FCCU Student Convenience App

  • Degree Audit System: A tool enabling students to track their academic progress, view completed and remaining courses, and ensure they meet graduation requirements.

  • Preference Input: Users can specify dietary restrictions, allergies, and favorite cuisines.

  • Recommendation Engine: Implemented a collaborative filtering algorithm for personalized recommendations.

Technologies: Python (Flask), Machine Learning (scikit-learn), HTML, CSS, JavaScript
